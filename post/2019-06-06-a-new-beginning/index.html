Is this Abyssaltech? Part-2 maybe?

 Posted on June 6, 2019  |  2 minutes (354 words)

Sometime around the same time yesteryear I put up a challenge to myself, summer vacations were approaching and I needed something to work on, that’s when I decided to make a small blog website, mostly because I wanted it to be something web and CS related.

I researched and learnt many things along the way, I set up the hosting and the domain and finally got it running. I managed to put together a small blog that I called Abyssal Tech (which apparently means deepest trences of technology) because I was going to post articles related to mainly technology.

I posted various articles, sparsely, whenever I got free time. They ranged from introductory technological topics like Intro to Quantum Computing to some computer science topics like Various files generated by Compilers and Deep Transfer Learning, a series of cybersecurity awareness posts like Are we really Secure? to some posts about personal interests like TV Shows - Cosmos: A Spacetime Odyssey and documentries - My favourite Documentries. There were some guide posts too that included GitHub on your Resume and Choosing Electives.

This all allowed me to garner a viewership that is far beyond my expectations and Google Search page ranking(by title) on page 1.

I myself learnt a lot from putting up stuff on here and I hope to continue in doing so.

The new name reflects what I truly envisioned this web-corner to be a box full of content for the technologically curious. Another year of science, computer science, technological, and personal posts inspired from whatever I do, read, want to read, and whatever I find out and learn on the interwebs.

A New Beginning

I intend to keep the layout and the overall feel same but posting a whole new bunch of posts on varied topics. I’ll always be about posting stuff that I do and never the other way round.
